Trust signal or warningWhat it detectsTypical action surfaced to userRelevant workflow

SIGNATURE_AMBIGUITY / FLAT_SIGNATURE_RISKReference signatures are highly similar or broad/flat, increasing exposure exchangeability.Report competing signatures and avoid overinterpreting fine-grained proportions.Advisor, trust, panel

THRESHOLD_DEPENDENTActive signature set changes materially across exposure thresholds.Report threshold sensitivity and use conservative interpretation.Single sample, cohort
HETEROGENEOUS_COHORTPairwise sample similarity suggests mixed processes or subgroups.Cluster or stratify before extraction or cohort-wide inference.Cohort
